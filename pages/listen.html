preface

i seem to have some commitment issues with music; i can't seem to say that i love it. i like it very much. but i see some people out there who listen to music so much more than i do, who could spend days living in their headphones that i can't help but hesitate in professing my love.

i used to worry about what people thought of the music i listened to. what a weird time.

songs that made me feel something

  • beneath the streetlights and the moon (jp cooper)
  • time i love to waste (may-a)
  • viva la vida (coldplay)
  • as the world caves in (matt maltese)
  • blue (taeyeon)

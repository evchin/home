preface

the word "interesting" feels like a fake word. nothing is truly interesting to me. there are too many things that are "interesting", which is why none of them are "interesting". i am interested in everything, so i am interested in nothing.

so when someone says something is interesting, i don't know what that means. what does that mean? what makes you interested in something and not interested in something else? because i think i could be interested in anything if it really came down to it.

so i think the real question is, what matters? what makes me feel truly alive and like life is something worth living? now that is an easy answer, folks.
